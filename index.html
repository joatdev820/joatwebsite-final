Mr. Jack of All Trades

Wilson Native Quinton Bryant found himself at a crossroads after being furloughed from his job at the beginning of the pandemic. A resourceful and experienced craftsman, Mr. Bryant applied his expansive knowledge of Carpentry and Home Improvement to grow Jack of all Trades of Wilson, NC LLC into a thriving company. Utilizing over 10 years of industy experience, Mr. Bryant started woodworking picture frames, inevitably evolving his business into a staffed team providing a variety services to Eastern NC area and beyond.
